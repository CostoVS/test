Fleet & Vehicle Tracking

Our fleet comprises of the following:

1. 18 ton tractors and trailers. The fleet is capable of transporting 8 ton, 20 ton and 34 ton loads to any destination.
2. Light delivery vehicles
3. Heavy trucks (A New range of M.A.N’s and Scania’s)
4. Our trailers consists of: o Inter-links, Super-links and other specialized trailers designed to meet the needs of our long term contracted clients.
5. Our fleet is professionally maintained by our workshop personnel on a 24 hour basis
6. We pride ourselves in maintaining a 24 hour in-house breakdown service to ensure we do not inconvenience our clients.
7. Our stores are well equipped with emergency repair kits and other components for our fleet. This enables us to repair our fleet in the case of on route breakdowns in the shortest possible time.

VEHICLE TRACKING

Every fleet vehicle is fitted with the C-Track fleet management system, with on-board voice capabilities. The system is monitored 24/7 by Forum SA operational staff, as well as the C-track control room. In addition, all drivers are issued with cellular phones for easier communication.
